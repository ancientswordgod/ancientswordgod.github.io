I'm a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at [University College London](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/), where I'm fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Jun Wang](http://www0.cs.ucl.ac.uk/staff/jun.wang/). My research interest lies broadly in multi-agent systems and the application of Large Language Models (LLMs) and agent-based methodologies to solve complex mathematical problems.

Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the [USTC–France Math Excellence Program](https://math.ustc.edu.cn/) at [University of Science and Technology of China](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/). I received my master's degree in Statistics from [Sorbonne University](https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/en).
